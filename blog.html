Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our courses, admissions, payments, and placements.

Admissions

What are the eligibility requirements?
Most beginner programs require basic computer literacy and English comprehension; advanced tracks may ask for prior portfolio or fundamental skills in design/marketing. Please check the course page for any additional prerequisites.
How do I apply for a course?
Click "Apply" on the course page, fill the short form, and choose your preferred start date. You'll receive an email with next steps and payment options once your seat is reserved.

Payments

Do you offer installments or EMI?
Yes, we support installment plans and common EMI options via major cards and UPI-based providers. Talk to our team to split fees across milestones without hidden charges.
Is my payment refundable?
Refunds are available before batch commencement minus an administrative fee. After classes begin, pro‑rata or deferrals may apply depending on attendance and materials access.

Learning & Tools

Are classes online or in‑person?
We run hybrid cohorts. Foundation modules can be attended online; studio and production sessions are in‑person for hands‑on practice with mentors.
Will I get recordings and resources?
Yes. Sessions are recorded whenever possible, and you'll get templates, briefs, datasets, project checklists, and feedback files in your student dashboard.

Placements

Do you provide placement assistance?
We support resume building, mock interviews, portfolio reviews, and referrals through partnered agencies and brands. Shortlisting depends on performance and portfolio quality.
How do I build a portfolio during the course?
Every module includes project briefs tied to real‑world scenarios. You'll finish with 4–8 polished case studies suitable for Behance/Dribbble/LinkedIn and recruiter review.

Can't find your answer? Contact our support team.
